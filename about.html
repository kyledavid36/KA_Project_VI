🚀 Project VI – About the Madness

Welcome to the About page for Project VI — our team's ambitious take on building a smarter elevator system.
In this project, we’re not just pressing buttons… we’re engineering the brains behind them. From CAN bus communication to real-time LED feedback, this elevator isn't just going up and down — it’s leveling up.
Seriously though, we’re proud of what we’re building and how we’re applying both software and hardware concepts to bring it to life.
This might not be Einstein’s E = mc2, but give us some time and a PCB or two — we’ll get there.
